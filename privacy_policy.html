Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Portfolio Application. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you as to how we look after your personal data when you use our application and tell you about your privacy rights.

2. Data We Collect

Our application uses Google Sign-In and the YouTube Data API. When you use these features, we request access to the following:

  • Basic Profile Information: (Name, Email, Profile Picture) to verify your identity.
  • YouTube Account Data: We access your YouTube subscriptions and playlists solely to display them within the app.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data strictly to:

  • Authenticate you via Google.
  • Display your subscribed channels and videos within the app interface.

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal data to outside parties. All data is processed locally on your device or directly via the YouTube API.

4. Data Retention

We do not store your Google/YouTube data on any external servers. The data is cached temporarily on your device for performance and is removed when you sign out or clear the app data.

5. Your Rights

You can revoke the app's access to your data at any time via your Google Account Permissions.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository.
